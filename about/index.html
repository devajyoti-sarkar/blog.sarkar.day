About

I am an award-winning author, entrepreneur, inventor and IT Executive. I have spent the last 14 years in a personal project to decipher the Indus script. The Indus Civilization was the largest of the four early civilizations - Egypt, Mesopotamia, China and Indus - but it is the only one we cannot read . It remains a mystery of Prehistory.

I will use this blog as a scratch pad to explore topics and build content for a future book that will disclose a full decipherment of the script.To do this I will post original translations of the Rig Veda as well as cover materials from the Indus, Egyptian and Mesopotamian civilizations.
